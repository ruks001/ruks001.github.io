Rukesh Prajapati

Research Assistant
Digital Media Computing Lab
Chosun University

I am a research assistant in Digital Media Computing Lab at Chosun University. The research in the lab is focused on the medical image analysis of brain magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) images for the diagnosis of Alzheimer's Disease (AD). My current area of research is deep reinforcement learning, generative adversarial network, and transformer models for the segmentation of brain tissue.
